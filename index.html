About Me

"Graphic design will save the world right after rock and roll does." - David Carson

Hello, I'm Alexis Smith

I've been creating digital content for as long as I can remember. It all started at home where I then took my love for design and decided that this is what I want to do for the rest of my life. I attended New York's finest design schools which gave me the tools and abilities that led me to working with major companies such as Google and Microsoft.

I've used design to solve many problems that I've been tasked with throughout my career. You can read my blog where I share my continously growing knowledge.
